Our 12-question diagnostic takes just 2 minutes and is based on established cognitive psychology frameworks. It assesses your learning style, optimal study times, retention patterns, and focus duration to create your unique cognitive profile.
The cognitive diagnostic is completely free - no sign-up required. You'll get your full cognitive profile and basic study recommendations at no cost. Premium features like the AI assistant, advanced analytics, and full Study Hub access are available with a subscription.
